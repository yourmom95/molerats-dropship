⚫♡have you ever drop shippped? probably not♡⚫

<>✧if you don't know what drop-shipping is then im going try and explain it and hopefully you'll understand:✧<>

⚫Drop shipping is a form of retail business where in the seller accepts customer orders without keeping stock on hand. But to give you a more simpler definition of drop-shipping it is basically when you don't want/need something anymore and you can sell it on an online website.

⚫♡do you know how to make money off drop-shipping? probably not♡⚫

<>✧also if you don't know how to make money off drop-shipping then i'll also explain it:✧<>

⚫When a customer purchases a product from a dropshipping store, a third-party supplier ships it directly to them. The customer pays the retail price you set, you pay the suppliers' wholesale price, and the rest is profit.
