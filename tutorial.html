    What dropshipping is!

  • Drop shipping is a form of retail business where in the seller accepts customer orders but does not keep goods sold in stock. Instead, in a form of supply chain management, it transfers the orders and their shipment details to either the manufacturer, a wholesaler, another retailer, or a fulfillment house, which then ships the goods directly to the customer.

    • How much a drop shipper makes!

  • In general, a dropshipper can earn between $1000- $50,000 per month or $100-$20,000 profit.
